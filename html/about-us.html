LEGACY

THEME

Today, we are faced with strenuous circumstances and serious predicaments on a global scale. One is witnessing the repeat of certain situations and eerie parallels between the 1900s and the 2000s, be it the economic recession, the pandemic, events leading up to war, or even the rise of social revolutions. History is definitely repeating itself.

But, what is new?
Mankind has evolved and so has his ability to tackle these situations. The solution to all destruction comes from our ability to innovate, our instinct to survive. Thus, even though the situations might be similar, we are confronting them with a fresher perspective and a renewed sense of realisation.


After all, we, at Arthanomics too, are Creating through the Chaos.

NOTE FROM CHAIRPERSONS

As two students who had admired Arthanomics for years, when we were given the opportunity to lead the fest and become a part of this remarkable legacy, we had a vision in mind for Arthanomics 2020. We wanted “our” Arthanomics to be unique and different, but above all, we had a vision to create something new.

However, as the world started facing one of its worst crises in history, all our plans and ideas quickly seemed to become redundant. But Arthanomics is nothing if not courageous, resilient, and most importantly, creative.

From collaborating with Indus Action, an NGO working to secure the food and health needs of communities that were severely affected during this lockdown - to getting used to having all our meetings over Zoom - to even redesigning our events to make them suitable to go online - our team is working relentlessly to make sure that we stay relevant. We turned our nightmare into our inspiration, declaring “Creation in Chaos” as our theme for 2020, and redefining what Arthanomics means to us. We are more than just a fest - Arthanomics is a youth-driven organization that allows innovative ideas to meet bold action, and our ideas will matter no matter what the medium of delivering them may be.

For this reason, we are very excited to be part of a new kind of legacy at Arthanomics, as we become the first-ever edition to go completely online to become ‘India’s First Online Business and Economics Fest’ and show the world what we have to offer.
