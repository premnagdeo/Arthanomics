Journalism in Jeopardy

×

Case Conundrum

×
If you're the kind of person who has an aptitude for conjuring up out-of-the-box solutions to seemingly difficult problems, step right up for the challenge.

Case Conundrum provides a platform for individuals to analyse and scrutinise case studies and provide effective and well thought-out solutions to both, general and specific problems faced by various companies.

Tackle real-world issues faced by companies in the realm of technology and innovation; pair up with another firm to develop and launch joint ventures; and, if you make it through to the final round, take a trip through time to draw inspiration from a historical company that survived an economic depression.

Participants per team: 1
Event Leads - Ashna Desai, Sarah Doctor

Comic Catalysis

×
Strokes of paint brushes, shades of colours and symmetrical and asymmetrical shapes in a picture is in itself a revolutionary movement more impactful than any political speech. Over the centuries fine arts has been a mirror reflecting a dynamic society.

"Caricatures", evolved in the 17th century, could be used to present a moral message or ridicule a corrupt leader. While today, "Memes" have become popular art created to critique and to satirise significant world events or simply even pop culture.

If you believe that you can challenge disparities in our society with your illustrative caricatures or witty memes, this is an event for you.

Participants per team: 1
Event Leads - Yash Mamtani, Himani Bakhda

The Snap Theory

×
Earth has become increasingly riddled with imbalance, poverty and disease. In this plague, the Mad Titan has arrived, to force onto us his warped paradise, a solution for when we face extinction. With the mightiest of them all vanquished in their quest to save the planet, the world now turns to you to deliver its destiny.

You can dread it, run from it, population growth arrives all the same. And now it is here.

To stop him, do you deem yourself fit? Or do you find yourself fighting on his side of the pit? Will you step over the wire to let your teammates pass over you, or will you cut the wire. And if you can’t save the world, you damn well avenge it.

Do whatever it takes, act fast. You're in the Endgame now.
